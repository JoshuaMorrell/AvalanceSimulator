Description

Avalanches are common yet unpredictable events that can leave any unknowing individual burried within seconds. Our project is a VR snow avalanche rescue simulator not meant to be a substitute for real-life rescue practice, but supplementary training to become more efficient and comfortable with the methods. It is intended to familiarize the user with how a real rescue would feel, and to help them practice proper search techniques.

History

With snow sports being one of the most popular backcountry activities, avalanches are inevitable risks that each individual must acknowledge prior to venturing into the mountains. Utah's Avalanche Center estimates that in the US, more people will recreate in the backcountry this year than ever befor. With COVID limiting in-person instructional avalanche rescue classes, simulations provide the opportunity for instructors to augment their online curriculum. By practicing rescues in VR, the intent would be that the user would be quicker, less panicked, and better prepared in a real rescue situation.

Features

  • Winter terrain equiped with adjustable snowfall and snow tracks
  • Hand held avalanche search and rescue equipments including beacon, snow probe, and shovel
  • Visual indicators for beacon and probing
